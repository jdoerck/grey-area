## Notes

- Gloss column uses bag-of-words word order (Marain order, English words) — not grammatically restructured.
- `ra'yuh` — first-person pronoun `ra` with spatial/positional modifier `yuh` (aboard/on). Compare: `geyuh` = second-person equivalent.
- `eseli` — appears to be a derived form; the base word in the vocabulary is `eselE` (attractive).
- This is a minimal seed set. Expand here as translations are developed.
